Welcome Eating Well

Tasty & Healthy was born out of the desire to make it easy to eat well, every day. After too many unfulfilling, unhealthy meals at the end of long work days, we were fed up. It felt like our only option were quick, greasy takeout or pricey restaurants... but there had to be a better way. So we set out to create it.

Tasty & Healthy was launched in March 2011 with the vision to usher in a new era of food without compromise. Since then, we've served nearly teo million meals, and we remain committed to providing honest food, made from scratch, delivered from our kitchen to your door.

Our Food

We recognize the benefits of promoting healthy products. Accessing healthy food is a challenge for many families. Small and large companies are demonstrating that what is good for the health of families is also good for business.
